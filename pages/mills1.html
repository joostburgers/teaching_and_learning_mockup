Lesson Plan

Preface

What does Faulkner think of his female characters? How does he write them? How does Faulkner’s world influence his writing of them? What does Faulkner think of Caddy?

This lesson is part one of a four-part lesson designed for a junior/senior elective English course at an all-boys independent high school. In order to fuel an in-class discussion, we will use Digital Yoknapatawpha to attempt to better understand Caddy’s world in The Sound and the Fury. This lesson can be in-person or synchronous online and is designed to take place after students have read all or most of the novel.
